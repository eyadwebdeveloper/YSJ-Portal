More Details:

Junior researchers are the mentees who are interested in learning scientific research. They work in groups to create research papers and contribute to their perspective fields. As a junior researcher, you are expected to:

- Attend biweekly research sessions
- Attend weekly individual sessions (optional) with their mentors to discuss their progress and help them apply what they have learned
- Submit tasks before their determined deadline

By the end of the program, each juniors team is expected to have finished their own research project. Papers fitting our guidelines will have the opportunity to be published in our journal.
