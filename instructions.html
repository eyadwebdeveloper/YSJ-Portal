Senior Researchers' Recruitment

Attention ladies and gentlemen!

Throughout the past two seasons of YSJ, we tried to make young researchers flourish with their ambitious ideas they have in life. For the sake of continuing such a success trailer, YSJ is announcing the official recruitment for the upcoming season's senior researchers. In YSJ seasons, senior researchers compromise an essential milestone that shortens the miles of effort required. If you are an expertise in research and would like to take the initiative being a mentor for youth research seekers, you came to the right place!

Job description:

- Mentor a four-junior researchers group in their first research intimation steps.
- Supervise additional 2 boot-camp research fellows.
- Scope with mentees throughout their papers' preparation.
